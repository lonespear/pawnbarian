# Chess Opening Explorer

An interactive Streamlit app to help you learn and practice your personalized chess opening repertoire.

## Your Repertoire

### As White:
- **Catalan Opening** (Closed & Open variations) - Your main weapon
- **Italian Game** (Giuoco Piano) - Backup for tactical games

### As Black vs 1.e4:
- **Caro-Kann Defense** (Classical & Advance variations)

### As Black vs 1.d4:
- **Queen's Gambit Declined** (Orthodox) - Solid main line
- **King's Indian Defense** - Attacking option

## How to Use

1. **Install dependencies:**
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```

2. **Run the app:**
   ```bash
   streamlit run chess_opening_app.py
   ```

3. **Navigate the app:**
   - Select an opening from the sidebar
   - Read the key ideas and strategic plans
   - Use the navigation buttons to step through moves
   - Watch the board update as you progress through the line

## Features

- ✅ Interactive chess board visualization
- ✅ Step-by-step move navigation
- ✅ Key ideas and strategic plans for each opening
- ✅ All main lines from your personalized repertoire
- ✅ Clean, easy-to-use interface

## Study Tips

- Focus on **understanding** over memorization
- Play **3 games** for every 1 hour of study
- Review your games - where did you deviate?
- Practice one opening for 2 weeks before moving to the next

## 8-Week Study Plan

| Week | Focus | Goal |
|------|-------|------|
| 1-2  | Catalan as White | Understand key plans in Closed & Open lines |
| 3-4  | Italian Game as White | Learn development and central play |
| 5-6  | Caro-Kann as Black | Master solid defensive setup |
| 7-8  | QGD & KID as Black | Learn both solid and dynamic options |

Good luck with your chess studies!
